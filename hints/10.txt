Exercise 10: Bitwise OR

OR compares bits pair by pair.
Result is 1 if EITHER (or both) bits is 1.

  1 OR 1 = 1
  1 OR 0 = 1
  0 OR 1 = 1
  0 OR 0 = 0

COMBINING VALUES:
OR is used to set bits or combine flags.

EXAMPLE:
  0x30 = 0011 0000
  0x05 = 0000 0101
  OR   = 0011 0101 = 0x35 = 53

THE PROBLEM:
Combine 0x30 and 0x05 to get 53.

TO COMPLETE:
Add this instruction:
  orq %rsi, %rdi

This ORs rsi into rdi: rdi = rdi | rsi = 0x30 | 0x05 = 0x35
